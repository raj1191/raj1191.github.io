About Us

The Pranic Wellness Centre

Pranasakthinilayam

The Pranasakthi Nilayam, is affiliated to Pranic Healing Trust Tamilnadu., Our Primary purpose is to teach, heal and promote Pranic Healing as a way to empower people to help heal themselves, improve their lives, help others and develop a greater connection or unity with their Divine nature, the Soul. The Pranasakthi Nilayam is located at Chennai. We are offering variety of services like Meditation, Healing, Training, Nurturing and so on. Our well professional healers are trained to handle all sort of illnesses with utmost love and care.
